# Business Decision Simulator Dashboard

## Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard that simulates how improvements in conversion
rate, average order value, and drop-off reduction impact future revenue.

## Key Features
- What-if sliders for business scenario simulation
- Projected revenue and revenue uplift calculation
- Conversion rate impact analysis
- Investment impact by product category
- Customer funnel (current state) visualization
- Revenue outlook: current vs projected

## Business Questions Answered
- How much additional revenue can be generated through conversion improvement?
- What is the impact of increasing average order value?
- Which product categories offer the highest investment return?
- How does reducing funnel drop-off affect revenue?

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- CSV dataset
